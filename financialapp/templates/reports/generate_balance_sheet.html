About Balance Sheets
What is a Balance Sheet?

A balance sheet is a financial statement that shows your financial position at a specific point in time. It follows the fundamental accounting equation: Assets = Liabilities + Net Worth.

  • Assets: What you own (cash, properties, investments)
  • Liabilities: What you owe (loans, debts, mortgages)
  • Net Worth: Your true wealth (Assets minus Liabilities)
Why is it Important?
  • Shows your current financial position
  • Helps track progress toward financial goals
  • Essential for loan applications and financial planning
  • Identifies debt levels and asset allocation
  • Provides basis for financial ratio analysis
Tip: Generate balance sheets regularly (monthly or quarterly) to track your financial progress.
